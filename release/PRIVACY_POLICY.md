# Somatiq Privacy Policy (MVP)

Last updated: 2026-03-04

## Summary

Somatiq is a local-first wellness app.  
All health data is processed and stored on your device.

## Data Collection

- Somatiq does not create user accounts.
- Somatiq does not send your health data to external servers.
- Somatiq does not use analytics, ad tracking, or telemetry.

## Health Data Access

Somatiq requests read-only Apple Health permissions for:
- Heart rate variability (SDNN)
- Heart rate and resting heart rate
- Sleep analysis
- Active energy burned
- Step count

Somatiq does not write data to Apple Health.

## Data Storage

- Data is stored locally via SwiftData on your device.
- If you delete the app, local app data is removed by iOS.

## Third Parties

Somatiq does not share personal health data with third-party services.

## Your Controls

- You can revoke Health permissions at any time in iOS Settings.
- You can delete app data by uninstalling the app.

## Medical Notice

Somatiq provides wellness insights only and is not a medical device.
