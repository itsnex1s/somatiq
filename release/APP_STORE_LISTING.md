# Somatiq App Store Listing Draft (MVP)

## App Name
Somatiq

## Subtitle
Private stress, sleep, and energy scores

## Promotional Text
Know your body. Own your data. Somatiq computes personalized wellness scores directly on your iPhone with no account and no cloud.

## Description
Somatiq turns Apple Health data into 3 simple daily scores:

- Stress Score (0–100)
- Sleep Score (0–100)
- Energy Score (0–100)

Built for privacy by default:
- 100% on-device processing
- No signup
- No telemetry
- No cloud required

What you can do:
- See today’s stress, sleep, and energy at a glance
- Understand your daily insight and key vitals
- Track 7/30/90-day trends
- Tune sleep target and personal preferences

Apple Health (read-only):
- HRV (SDNN)
- Heart rate and resting heart rate
- Sleep analysis
- Active energy and steps

Important:
Somatiq is a wellness app and not a medical device.
